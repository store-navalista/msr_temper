Newbuilding Classification

We provide full support throughout the shipbuilding process — from the approval of design documentation to the final commissioning. Our mission is to ensure full compliance with international standards, class society requirements, and flag state regulations.

More Details

Periodic Surveys & Certification

Regular surveys are essential for the safe and lawful operation of any vessel. We conduct periodic inspections in line with class and statutory requirements, ensuring continued compliance and readiness for sea.

More Details

Structural Assessment

We carry out detailed structural assessments to evaluate the vessel's condition, identify critical areas of concern, and provide recommendations for repair or reinforcement. These evaluations are key to extending service life and ensuring safety at sea.

More Details

Risk-Based Inspection Plan

Our Risk-Based Inspection plans are tailored to each vessel’s design, history, and operating profile. By focusing on critical systems and potential failure points, RBI allows for a more efficient, targeted inspection strategy.
